Acceleration

Exploding from a standstill, the most useful speed in rugby. Tap each drill to see how.

Golden ruleSprint fresh. When the sprints slow down, you're done.
Main work
Rest fully between every sprint. Walk back slowly, 60 to 90 seconds. No rush.

Finish

Two relaxed run-throughs at ~80% over 30 m, feeling smooth. Then a 5-minute easy jog & walk to cool down.
